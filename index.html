About Me

I am a graphic design student in my first year at North Idaho College. I am originally from the San Francisco Bay Area and moved to north Idaho about 4 years ago. Even though I miss the city you really cannot beat the fresh air. Plus, the program that I am attending here has made me reach goals that I never though I could have. I have learned to be confident in the aspects of design through constant learning and curiosity. The way I see it, everyone still has a lot to learn, even the seasoned veterans. My goal after my first year is to find an internship and work relentlessly there, gathering all of the knowledge that I can. I hope to work as a graphic designer using software like Illustrator and Photoshop to create logos and advertisements among other things.
